Scryptian - Privacy Notice
--------------------------

Scryptian is free and runs AI locally on your PC - no subscription,
no OpenAI, no account required.

Some skills use external services (e.g. Google Translate for translation).
All other skills process your text entirely on your machine.

Scryptian collects anonymous usage stats (which skills are used, error
reports) to fix bugs and improve the app. No personal data, no account.

Source code is open and auditable:
https://github.com/adrianium/Scryptian
